reification

Reification, to Georgy Lukacs and other Western Marxists, is a special kind of Alienation in which social relations are perceived as inherent attributes of people, and where both subject and object's roles are replaced and interchangeable.

To Lukacs, the Commodity Form serves as the basis for all capitalist objectivity, and tends to reduce qualitative distinctions between people and things into quantitative categories
