Historikerstreit

Historians' dispute over the nature of Nazism and its role in German history

Historians involved

  • Hugh Trevor-Roper
  • Ernst Nolte
  • Andreas Hillgruber, who believed that Allied policies in 1944 and 1945 were no different from those of the Germans
  • Richard J. Evans
  • Hans-Ulrich Wehler, the liberal historian who believed Germany had only modernized partially in the 1800s and retained largely medieval values
