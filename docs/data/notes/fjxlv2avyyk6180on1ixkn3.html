Althusserian Theory of Ideology

In contrast to the classical Marxist understanding of false consciousness, which Althusser believed as mistakenly rooted in idealism, Althusser believes that ideological state apparatuses exist within society existed to enforce compulsory beliefs through obedience to dogma, stating that ideology has a material existence.
